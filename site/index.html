Switch Discord voice channels with your controller

A tiny, controller-first overlay for joining, switching, and controlling Discord voice channels — without ever pulling the full Discord client into view.

Download latest release View on GitHub

What it does

🎮 Built for a gamepad

Navigate, join, mute, deafen, and adjust volume entirely from a controller. Runs great as a Steam "Non-Steam Game" so the overlay works mid-match.

🗂️ Just your voice channels

Channels grouped by server, with pinned favorites first and the rest ranked by how often you actually use them.

🪟 Stays out of the way

A compact, always-on-top window you toggle with a controller chord or hotkey — no alt-tabbing into the full Discord app.

🔒 Local and private

No servers, no analytics, no tracking. Everything stays on your machine and talks only to your own Discord client.

⬇️ Auto-updates

The app checks GitHub for new releases and surfaces them in-app, so you stay on the latest version.
